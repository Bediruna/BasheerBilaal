Imam Basheer Bilaal

Born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. Coming from a family of Imams and scholars, he is a graduate of the University of Islamic Studies with various Ijaazaat as an Imam and khatib from the Presidency of Religious Affairs in Turkey.

He has served in many communities in the US including; The United American Muslim Association of New York, The Islamic Society of Central Delaware, The Islamic Society of Central Florida, The Islamic Society of Greater Lowell. He has also served as the muslim representative to the White house on countering violent extremism for 4 years. Currently serving as the imam of the Muslim Association of Lehigh Valley.
