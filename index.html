Imam Besir Bilal (Basheer Bilaal)

Born raised in Brooklyn, New York. Coming from a long line of Imams and scholars, like his father, he too graduated the University of Islamic Studies as an Imam and khatib from the Presidency of Religious Affairs in Turkey.

Although a young Imam, he has already served in many communities in the US including; The United American Muslim Association of New York, The Islamic Society of Central Delaware, and The Islamic Society of Central Florida. Currently working as Imam of the Islamic Society of Greater Lowell, serving the diverse Islamic community with its religious/spiritual needs and working to serve the community to spread peace, knowledge & understanding.
